Our Services

We offer a range of services to help you achieve your health and fitness goals:

  • Activity Tracking

    Our app allows you to easily track your daily activities, such as walking distance, steps, and calories burned, so you can monitor your progress and stay motivated.

  • Goal Setting

    Set your fitness goals, such as daily steps target or distance to be covered, and our app will help you stay on track and achieve your goals with regular progress updates.

  • Insights and Reports

    Get valuable insights and reports on your health and fitness activities, such as daily, weekly, and monthly summaries.

  • User Management

    As an admin, you can easily manage user accounts through CRUD operations. Add, edit, or delete user details to keep your user database up-to-date and secure.

  • Email Notifications

    Our app automatically sends email notifications to the top 10 users who achieve their daily goals, to appreciate and encourage their progress.
